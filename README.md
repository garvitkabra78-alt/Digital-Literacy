Task 5: Cybercrime Awareness and Financial Security

The final task was a deep dive into the mechanics of UPI (Unified Payments Interface) fraud, a rapidly growing cybercrime that exploits human psychology rather than technical flaws.
•	Anatomy of a Scam: Scammers often create a sense of urgency regarding KYC updates or failed deliveries to trick users into approving "collect requests" or installing screen-sharing apps.
•	Target Demographics: The study identified students, the elderly, and small business owners as the most common targets.
•	Prevention Protocols: Critical safety habits were established, such as the rule that receiving money never requires entering a UPI PIN.
•	Incident Reporting: In the event of fraud, individuals are encouraged to report to the National Cyber Crime Portal or call the helpline at 1930 for immediate assistance.
